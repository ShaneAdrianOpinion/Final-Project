About Us

DeVanNess Delights Corp. is a SEC registered company owned by the the couple, Evanie Cagatin, RN and Dennise Cagatin, CPA, who we're a former OFW in Saudi Arabia.

In 2019, they decided to build their own milktea business.
The main store is located at Poblacion Occidental, Valencia, Bohol.
They offer the best milktea in the town which the customers love and enjoy.
A year after, they have reached almots 40 franchises inside Bohol.
They offer catering services, milktea training, lowest franchise and starter pack promo. In 2021, they started new branch at Old Airport Tagbilaran and launched the first ever DeVanNess Mobile Milktea at Jagna,Bohol.
